Digital Development Principles and the Caribbean Public Sector
20.08.15
On Tuesday August 25th, Matthew McNaughton will be presenting on the "Principles for Digital Development" at the Caribbean Technology Innovation and Digital Economy (TIDE) Conference in Port of Spain, Trinidad & Tobago. The Principles are "living" guidelines on the best practices for creating technology-enabled programmes. You can see Matthew's presentation here and learn about the Principles on its website.


NEWS
Improving Farmer's Access to Information at the Africa Open Data Conference
02.09.15
During the first week of September Matthew McNaughton will be in Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania to share SlashRoots' work on Open Data and Agriculture in the Caribbean. On Wednesday September 3rd he will be presenting a lightening talk "Towards a data-driven Agriculture sector in Jamaica" at the Global Open Data for Agricultural and Nutrition (GODAN) Partner meeting, and on Saturday September 5th participating in the GODAN workshop discussing how to increase Farmers' access to information using Open Data.
EVENT
StartUp Jam Series:
Ben Cole of Kickstarter
28.10.15
The next workshop in the StartupJAM entrepreneurial series will take place on Wednesday, October 28, 2015 under the theme "How to build an airtight product strategy" The guest speaker for the workshop is Ben Cole, Product Manager at Kickstarter. Ben Cole is a leader who cares deeply about the social impact of technology. He is currently a product manager at Kickstarter, advisor to the Clinton campaign, and product management instructor at General Assembly.
